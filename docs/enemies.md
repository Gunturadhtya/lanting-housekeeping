# Enemies

Enemies (in-game: _musuh_) are entities that spawn in waves and try to destroy the player's [lanting house](lanting-house.md). They spawn from the top of the screen and move toward the lanting house. Once they're close enough to the lanting house, they will start attacking it.

Enemies ignore the player's units unless the units are in their line of path. In that case, the enemies will attack the units.
